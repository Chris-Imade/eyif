About EYIF

Empowered Youth,
Transformed Future

Youth constitutes the backbone of our state's development. With rising unemployment and underemployment, young people must be inspired and equipped with practical skills, entrepreneurial thinking, and civic awareness. This event seeks to rally national attention on youth empowerment, showcasing innovation, entrepreneurship, and civic participation. The presence of key dignitaries adds significant credibility, visibility, and national attention to the forum.
